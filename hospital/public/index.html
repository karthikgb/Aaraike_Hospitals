Welcome to Our Hospitals

Aaraike Hospitals

Aaraike Hospitals, a 100 bedded Multispecialty is one of the most acclaimed health care facilities which has gained recognition through unwavering commitment towards providing compassionate care and Healing. The chain was originally founded by Xenon Critical Care Companions and is centrally located at Malakpet. The numerous years of combined experience helps Aaraike Hospitals serve patients with dedication and precision. Medicine is a rapidly changing field, with rapid advances coming in, with more and more focused fields and interdisciplinary fields evolving like Emergency Medicine, Critical Care, Pain Medicine, Family Medicine, Geriatric Care, Diabetic Care, Laparoscopic Surgeries, Arthroscopi c Surgeries, Joint Surgeries, Spine Surgeries, Infertility etc. We are proud to introduce all the above new disciplines in our Hospitals. We have highly qualified doctors providing 24 hr coverage on all days with wide range of specialties apart from the new fields mentioned above like General Medicine, Pediatrics, Pulmonology, Nephrology, Neurology, Gastroenterology , Endocrinology, Medical Oncology, Psychiatry, General Surgery, Obstetrics and Gynecology, Orthopedic Surgery, Neurosurgery, Plastic Surgery, Surgical Gastroenterology, Surgical Oncology etc. which are required to solve day to day health care problems.
We are equipped with 6 bedded, state-of-the-art emergency care unit and ambulance services to take care of all emergencies and trauma patients.
